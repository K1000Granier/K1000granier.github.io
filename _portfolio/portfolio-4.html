--- title: "Collisionless Plasmoid Instability" excerpt: "Collisionless plasmoid instability in guide-field reconnection.
" collection: portfolio --- In this work, we study the collisionless plasmoid instability in reconnecting current sheets with a strong guide field. The main goal is to identify the conditions under which a collisionless sheet becomes unstable and breaks into plasmoids, and to determine how finite-\(\beta_e\) and electron-scale effects modify this threshold.

Using fluid, gyrofluid, and gyrokinetic approaches, this work shows how collisionless effects control current-sheet stability and the onset of plasmoid-mediated reconnection.
